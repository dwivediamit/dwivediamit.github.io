Who we are?

The Company was founded with the aim of promoting of Indian spices and foodstuff for domestic market and international market. KIA Trading venture specialising in the manufacturing and supply of a large variety of food grains, flour , masalas and pulses to distributors, large retailers, Hotels, Restaurants, Caterers, branded supermarkets, to wholesalers and available for export market across the globe.

Owing to the legacy, stringent quality standards in food processing and relationship management, KIA Trading has earned its position as the most sought after brand by consumers, restaurateurs and retailers alike.

Our Vision

Across the globe, KIA Trading is committed to offer its premium products to its customers with food safety while making sure that all public health requirements are fulfilled. To accomplish this mission we have invested highly equipped infrastructure unit, facilitated with enhanced state of art technology in accordance with HACCP/ISO specification

Our Mission

Continuous enhancements in quality of food processing and packing, through adoption of technology, and to strengthen the distribution network to ensure availability, visibility and affordability
