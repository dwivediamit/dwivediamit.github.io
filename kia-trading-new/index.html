Welcome to KIA Trading

We are a Technology- based, quality- driven & customer -oriented worldwide supplier of a complete range of authentic & finest products.

We take pride in being the suppliers of all types of Food products to world’s leading food multinationals & fast food chains in the Europe, Middle East & Asia.

Trading is managed by principles of value creation & we add value to our customers by supplying innovative products and services based on our in-depth understanding of our customer needs and requirements. We at KIA Trading are a dedicated team and together we are committed to achieve our goals by providing Quality products blended with world class customer service
